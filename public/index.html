How to play CodiNames

  1. The aim of the game is to determine, with help of your spymaster, which cards belong to your team.
  2. PLlayers are divided into 2 teams of equal size and roles are randomly placed throughout the board
  3. The roles are red team, blue team, bystander, and assassin
  4. The spymaster will take turns giving a clue which consists of a word and a number, where the number refers the number of cards that the clue refers to
  5. You may not give a clue that is a word that is on, or consists of a word on the table
  6. Upon hearing a clue from their spymaster, the team will guess choose one of the cards on the table
  7. Once a card is picked, it flips over and one of four things can happen:
      If it is a card of your own colour, you may take another turn
      If it is a card of the other teams colour, it becomes their turn
      If it is an innocent bystander, it also becomes the other teams turn
      If it is the assassin, your team loses the game
