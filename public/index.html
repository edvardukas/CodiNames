How to play CodiNames

  • The aim of the game is to determine, with help of your spymaster, which cards belong to your team
  • Players are divided into 2 teams of equal size and roles are randomly placed throughout the board
  • The roles are red team, blue team, bystander, and assassin
  • The spymaster will take turns giving a clue which consists of a word and a number, where the number refers the number of cards that the clue refers to
  • You may not give a clue that is a word that is on, or consists of a word on the table
  • Upon hearing a clue from their spymaster, the team will guess choose one of the cards on the table
  • Once a card is picked, it flips over and one of four things can happen:
    1. If it is a card of your own colour, you may take another turn
    2. If it is a card of the other teams colour, it becomes their turn
    3. If it is an innocent bystander, it also becomes the other teams turn
    4. If it is the assassin, your team loses the game
  • Repeat this until either one team loses, or until all the cards of one teams colour have been turned over
  • If your team has turned over all of its cards, your team wins
